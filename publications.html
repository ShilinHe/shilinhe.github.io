Research Interest:

I am a Ph.D candidate at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Michael R. Lyu. My research concerns the interpretability-driven intelligent software reliability engineering, including intelligent log analysis and neural machine translation interpretation.

Our project LogPAI provides an end-to-end solution to intelligently manage and analyze logs for modern software systems using machine learning techniques. So far, the project has received 2000+ stars and 800+ forks and the datasets Loghub have been downloaded for more than 19000 times by 370+ organizations. Feel free to contact us if you have any questions.

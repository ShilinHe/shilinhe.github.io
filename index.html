About

Shilin He is now an Applied Scientist in Bytedance Douyin, working on AI4SE and AI Coding. Prior to that, He was a Senior Researcher in the DKI group @ Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) during 2020-2025. He received the Ph.D. degree from the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) under the supervision of Prof. Michael R. Lyu in 2020. He is the program committee member of top-tier conferences like FSE and ISSTA, and reviewer of conferences and journals such as ACL, EMNLP, FSE, TSE, TOSEM.

About

Shilin He is now working as an Applied Scientist in Bytedance Douyin on AI4SE and AI Coding directions. Prior to that, He was a Senior Researcher in Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) during 2020-2025. He received the Ph.D. degree from the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) under the supervision of Prof. Michael R. Lyu in 2020.

Shilin's research interests include AI4SE, LLM, Agent, and etc. He has published over 40 papers on top-tier conference and journal papers such as ICLR/ACL and ICSE/FSE, with a total citation of 5000+. He was the program committee member of top-tier conferences like FSE and ISSTA, and reviewer of conferences and journals such as ACL/EMNLP, and FSE/TSE/TOSEM.
